## Submission Boundaries

Submit or publish only from official Nayovi, founder, or developer-owned accounts. If a platform requires signup, CAPTCHA, phone/email verification, identity upload, terms acceptance, or private login, mark `OWNER_ACTION_REQUIRED`.

Skip a listing or pitch when the platform requires:

- Paid placement as a backlink acquisition tactic.
- Reciprocal homepage links.
- Mirror-first APK download pages.
- Fake user reviews or upvote requests.
- Misleading free, open-source, or official-store claims.
