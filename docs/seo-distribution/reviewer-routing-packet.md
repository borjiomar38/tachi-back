## Routing Rules

Use the download page when the reviewer needs the official APK source, pricing path, support path, or Android verification status language.

Use the OCR checklist when the reviewer wants a product-light resource about text-region coverage, reading order, bubble grouping, glossary drift, human correction, and screenshot boundaries.

Use the approved-sample pilot guide when the reviewer, creator platform, localization studio, or partner asks how Nayovi avoids unauthorized chapter processing.

Use the hosted workflow page when a directory, investor, or commercial partner asks for the business workflow, paid-token plan context, review-code routing, or attribution separation.

Do not route any reviewer to a mirror-first APK page, paid-link package, scraped contact list, fake review request, private analytics export, unapproved screenshot set, or profile that the owner has not confirmed as official.

## Owner Confirmation Needed

- Package name and signing-certificate fingerprint.
- Current APK hash and whether it may be public.
- Android developer verification and package registration status.
- Approved screenshot/demo asset references.
- Public media contact and quote policy.
- Review-code policy and whether third-party reports may cite Nayovi.
