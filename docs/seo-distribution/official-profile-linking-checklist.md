# Nayovi Official Profile Linking Checklist

This checklist is for owner-created Nayovi profiles, directory listings, reviewer packets, and official social accounts. It keeps public trust signals consistent without storing credentials or posting automatically.

## Canonical Links

- Primary brand domain: `https://nayovi.com`
- Primary product and APK source: `https://tachiyomiat.com/download`
- AI translation landing page: `https://tachiyomiat.com/translate-manhwa-ai`
- Pricing: `https://tachiyomiat.com/pricing`
- Support: `https://tachiyomiat.com/support`
- Responsible workflow: `https://tachiyomiat.com/guides/translation-support-workflow`
- Neutral OCR checklist: `https://tachiyomiat.com/guides/comic-ocr-translation-checklist`
- Takedown and official-source policy: `https://tachiyomiat.com/legal/official-sources-takedown`

## Profile Copy

Short bio:
Nayovi is an Android APK and hosted OCR/AI translation workflow for manga, manhwa, and manhua reader workflows.

Responsible-use line:
Nayovi does not host or distribute chapters; it supports owned content, public-domain material, official samples, or content users have permission to process.

Directory note:
Use the official APK/download URL as the product link. Do not use mirror-first APK URLs as the primary source, and do not accept reciprocal-link or paid-link placement requirements without owner approval.

## Asset Checklist

- Square logo/avatar from the current Nayovi brand asset.
- Banner or screenshot that shows product UI or Android workflow without copyrighted chapter pages.
- One official demo video or poster when the platform supports media.
- Pricing, support, privacy, terms, and responsible-use links visible from the profile or listing.
- Package name, SHA-256, signing-certificate fingerprint, and Android developer verification status once confirmed by the owner.

## Credential Handling

- Store only credential variable names in docs.
- Store actual values in `/opt/tachi-back/.env.seo-distribution-agent` with strict permissions or another approved secret store.
- Common references: `SEO_AGENT_LINKEDIN_ACCESS_TOKEN`, `SEO_AGENT_LINKEDIN_ORGANIZATION_ID`, `SEO_AGENT_YOUTUBE_REFRESH_TOKEN`, `SEO_AGENT_PRODUCTHUNT_TOKEN`, `SEO_AGENT_DEVTO_API_KEY`, `SEO_AGENT_MEDIUM_INTEGRATION_TOKEN`, `SEO_AGENT_REDDIT_CLIENT_ID`, `SEO_AGENT_REDDIT_CLIENT_SECRET`, `SEO_AGENT_REDDIT_REFRESH_TOKEN`, `SEO_AGENT_X_ACCESS_TOKEN`.

## Manual Review Gates

- Account creation, email or phone verification, CAPTCHA, identity checks, and terms acceptance are owner actions.
- Social posting stays draft-only until an authorized official account/API workflow is configured.
- Community links are omitted unless current rules clearly allow the exact post and the link is useful in context.
