## Eligibility Gates

Use this gate before any AlternativeTo, F-Droid, IzzyOnDroid, APK directory, mobile app directory, or resource-list action.

Allowed only after owner approval:
- Official founder/company submitter identity.
- Current package name, SHA-256, signing-certificate fingerprint, and Android developer verification status.
- Approved screenshots or demo clips that use only permission-safe samples.
- Clear pricing label: free trial or redeem-code access plus paid monthly token plans.
- Source-link preservation: official APK, support, pricing, privacy, terms, and responsible-use links remain visible.
- No mirror-first redistribution unless the owner explicitly approves the exact platform and public wording.

Reject or hold:
- Listings that require reciprocal links, paid backlinks, hidden redirects, fake reviews, vote requests, or artificial engagement.
- Forms that force Nayovi to be labeled forever-free, open-source, app-store-only, generic chatbot, or chapter-hosting software.
- Open-source repositories or stores that require FLOSS licensing unless Nayovi has a real qualifying open-source component.
- Any path that requires automated signup, CAPTCHA, email/phone verification, identity documents, terms acceptance, or third-party login.
