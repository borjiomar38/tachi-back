## Public Description

Nayovi is an Android APK and hosted OCR/AI translation workflow for manga, manhwa, and manhua reader workflows. It supports official APK access, free trial or redeem-code activation, monthly token plans, and support paths.

Nayovi does not host or distribute chapters. Testing, screenshots, demos, and reviews should use owned material, public-domain works, official samples, creator-provided pages, or content the tester has permission to process.
