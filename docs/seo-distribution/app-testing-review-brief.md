# Nayovi App Testing and Review Brief

Status: owner review required.

This brief is for official app-testing portals, Android reviewers, APK directories, and partner diligence teams. It is not permission to upload the APK, mirror files, request ratings, buy reviews, or publish screenshots. Use it only after the owner approves the portal, test scope, sample material, and any public report permission.

## Product Context

Nayovi is an Android APK and hosted OCR/AI translation workflow for manga, manhwa, and manhua reader workflows.

Nayovi does not host or distribute chapters. Testing should use owned pages, public-domain material, official samples, creator-provided samples, or content the tester has written permission to process.

## Test Scope

Useful test areas:

- Official download-page clarity and source-of-truth links.
- Install flow, Android permission prompts, and first-run behavior.
- Free trial or redeem-code activation path.
- OCR upload or page-processing progress states.
- Translation loading, retry, and support handoff.
- Pricing and token-plan clarity.
- Privacy, terms, support, and responsible-use visibility.
- Whether screenshots avoid unauthorized chapters, private data, payment details, device identifiers, and private codes.

Do not test with scraped chapters, paywalled pages, scanlation uploads, private user libraries, or content where screenshot/publication rights are unclear.

## Public Report Rules

Any public review, report, listing, video, or directory entry should:

- Cite the official APK URL rather than a mirror.
- Keep pricing accurate as free trial or redeem-code access plus paid monthly token plans.
- Keep package name, signing fingerprint, Android developer verification status, and package registration status pending unless the owner supplies exact public language.
- Use approved sample screenshots only.
- Avoid fake ratings, paid-positive-review wording, review swaps, vote requests, and dofollow reciprocal backlink requirements.
- Avoid calling Nayovi forever-free, open-source, app-store-only, a chapter source, or an official manga catalog.

## Owner Approval Gate

Before any app-testing or review portal action, the owner must confirm:

- Current APK filename, build label, size, and SHA-256.
- Package name, signing-certificate fingerprint, developer verification status, and package registration state, or approved pending-language copy.
- Whether the APK may be uploaded to the portal or only tested from the official download page.
- Whether a redeem code, trial account, or reviewer code may be provided.
- Which sample pages and screenshots are approved for testing and publication.
- Whether any public report may cite Nayovi, and under which source-link/no-mirror rules.
